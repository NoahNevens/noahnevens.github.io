Mario Super Sluggers

One day noah and his friends congregated for a Mario Super Sluggers tournament. Before they began playing, each player took turns drafting the best team possible. However, everyone had trouble remembering which characters had been taken and which were available. Over the course of the next week, noah created a solution using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript whereby each person could keep track of their team and their team's stats.

Eleven

Eleven is a simple logic game whereby a player uses a control panel, a collection of resources and their own wits to evade and combat enemies in an arena. The goal is to grab enough trophies in the arena to advance from level to level without losing all of your health. This project uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
